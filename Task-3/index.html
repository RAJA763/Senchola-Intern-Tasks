ABOUT US

Kerala, located in southwestern India, is a coastal state known as "God's Own Country." It boasts diverse landscapes, including backwaters, beaches, hills, and forests. Rich in cultural heritage, Kerala is famous for traditional art forms and vibrant festivals. The state is also a hub for Ayurvedic wellness treatments, attracting wellness enthusiasts from around the world. With its warm hospitality and delectable cuisine, Kerala offers a captivating and immersive experience for travelers seeking a blend of natural beauty and cultural charm.

Alleppey

This coastal town is famous for its backwater cruises, serene beaches, and vibrant houseboat experiences. It hosts the Nehru Trophy Boat Race, a popular event that attracts tourists from around the world.
